Trader Joe's Orange Chicken

Ingredients:

  • One bag of Trader Joe's Orange Chicken
  • 1 tbsp oil

Directions:

  1. Heat your skillet with 1 tbsp oil to medium heat
  2. Open the broccoli bag and empty its contents into the skillet
  3. Cook until soft
  4. Remove broccoli from skillet
  5. Turn the stove up to medium/high heat. Place beef into the skillet
  6. Heat until cooked through and golden brown
  7. Put broccoli back into the skillet with the beef
  8. Empty sauce packets into the skillet
  9. Cook for 1-3 minutes
  10. Serve and enjoy
