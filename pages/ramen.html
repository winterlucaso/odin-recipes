Shin Ramen Noodles

This ramen recipe has been passed down from generation to generation.

Ingredients:

  • One packet of Shin Ramen Noodles
  • 2 eggs

Directions:

  1. Fill skillet with about 1 inch of water from the bottom. Heat skillet on high until boiling.
  2. Empty the contents of the packet including the noodles and both spice packets
  3. Cook about 5 minutes or until tender
  4. Crack 2 eggs directly into the skillet with the noodles
  5. Stir occasionally, ensuring that the eggs whites cook fully
  6. Serve into a bowl and enjoy
