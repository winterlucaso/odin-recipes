Signature Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup pancake mix
  • Sprinkles

Directions:

  1. Heat skillet at medium heat with 1/2 tbsp of butter
  2. Meanwhile, put 1 cup pancake mix into a mixing bowl
  3. Put about 3/4 cup of water into the bowl. Mix. Add water until until mix becomes vicous
  4. Pour the mixture onto the skillet until it has a diameter of about 3 inches
  5. Sprinkle a pinch of sprinkles onto the pancakes
  6. After about 1 minute, use a spatula to check the progress. Flip once golden brown
  7. Check again after about 1 minute, and remove from skillet when ready
  8. Serve with butter and syrup. Enjoy.
